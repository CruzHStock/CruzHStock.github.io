PROGRAM NOTES
The dark is simply a scary place. What is within its grasp, is it something alive that seems always to be lurking and watching? What is it within the dark that always seems to echo us? Is there any way to traverse the darkness and make it to the light unscathed? If the darkness is or was never a threat, then why do children run from it?
